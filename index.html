The Maker Story

At Cuppa City, we are excited about the process, taking the scenic route to get there. We’ll keep our eyes on the coffee at all times. We’ll tell you what we can deliver, and little else.

About Nell :Founder of Cuppa City, I am a barista and coffee roaster. That means I can roast freshly harvested green coffee beans and brew it into a delicious beverage. I honed my skills in espresso brewing techniques working as barista in a specialty coffee shop in Seattle. As a SCA* certified coffee roaster, I have in-depth knowledge of coffee bean analysis and taste evaluation.*Speciality Coffee Association
